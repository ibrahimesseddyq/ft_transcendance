## Compensation & Financial Benefits

### Base Salary
* Competitive market-aligned salary
* Reviewed annually during performance cycle

### Performance Bonus

* Annual performance-based bonus Based on:
    * Individual performance
    * Team contribution
    * Company financial performance
* Bonus range: 5%–20% of annual salary

### Referral Bonus
* Employees receive bonus for successful candidate referrals

## Health & Insurance

* Comprehensive health insurance
* Coverage includes:
    * General medical care
    * Specialist visits
    * Emergency services
* Partial dental coverage
* Annual health check allowance

## Learning & Development

### Learning Budget
* Annual learning budget per employee can be used for:
    * Online courses
    * Certifications
    * Technical books
    * Conferences

### Internal Knowledge Sharing
* Monthly tech talks
* Architecture review sessions
* Peer mentorship

### Certification Support
* Company sponsors relevant professional certifications
* Example areas:
    * Cloud
    * DevOps
    * Backend technologies

## Work Model & Flexibility

### Work Type
* Hybrid work model
    * 3 days remote
    * 2 days office

### Flexible Hours
* Core hours: 10:00–15:00
* Remaining hours flexible between 08:00–18:00

### Remote Work Policy
* Fully remote allowed for senior roles upon approval
* Remote from abroad requires management approval

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Annual Leave
* 20 working days per year
* Accrued monthly

### Sick Leave
* Paid sick leave according to local labor law
* Medical certificate required after 2 consecutive days

### Public Holidays
* All national public holidays observed

### Personal Leave
* Up to 3 paid personal days per year

## Career Progression Policy

* Promotion is based on:
    * Technical impact
    * Leadership contribution
    * Code quality consistency
    * Ownership and accountability
* Promotion review cycle:
    * Twice per year
* Internal mobility:
    * Employees can apply to internal roles after 12 months

## Interview & Reapplication Policy

### Reapplication Rule
* Candidates can reapply after 6 months
* Feedback is provided when possible

### Fair Evaluation
* All candidates evaluated using structured criteria
* Interview scoring rubric is standardized

### Equal Opportunity
* Hiring decisions are based solely on:
    * Skills
    * Experience
    * Interview performance
* The company does not discriminate based on:
    * Gender
    * Religion
    * Nationality
    * Disability
    * Marital status

## Data Privacy & Security

* All candidate data is stored securely
* Data is not shared externally
* Interview recordings are:
    * Stored securely
    * Access limited to authorized HR and hiring managers
* Candidates may request data deletion after process completion

## Workplace Conduct Policy

* Employees and candidates must:
    * Maintain respectful communication
    * Avoid discriminatory or offensive language
    * Follow company ethical standards
* Harassment or inappropriate behavior may result in:
    * Interview termination
    * Disciplinary action

## Code of Ethics

* The company enforces:
    * Transparency in decision-making
    * Confidential handling of sensitive data
    * Clear conflict-of-interest policies
    * Anti-corruption standards
